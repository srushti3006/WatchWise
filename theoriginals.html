The Originals reviews




1] VampireLoreQueen:
The Originals is honestly one of the best supernatural dramas 
I’ve ever watched. The Mikaelson family is chaotic, tragic, and 
lovable all at once. Klaus’ character development is absolute 
perfection—watching him struggle between being a monster and a 
father hit me right in the heart. The writing feels rich, the New 
Orleans setting is magical, and the soundtrack? Chef’s kiss. This 
show gave me everything I wanted and more.



2]CasualBingeWatcher:
I enjoyed The Originals, but not as much as I expected to after 
watching The Vampire Diaries. Some episodes were slow, and the politics 
between the witches, vampires, and werewolves sometimes felt repetitive. 
BUT I’ll admit the action scenes were great, and Elijah carried the 
entire show with his elegance and calm brutality. Overall solid, but 
not a must-watch for me.



3]TheoTheCritic
Honestly? Not my cup of tea. The show is heavy on melodrama and endless
 family speeches that got tiring fast. I kept waiting for a real plot 
twist, but many of the conflicts felt recycled. The performances were 
strong, but the story didn’t grip me. Fans of supernatural soap operas 
will definitely enjoy this, but I couldn't fully connect.



4]NOLAAddict
Absolutely adored it. As someone who LOVES New Orleans, the atmosphere 
and culture the show captured made me fall in love instantly. The supernatural 
lore mixed with the city's history was brilliant. Rebekah is my favorite 
character of all time—she deserved the world! I rewatch the series every 
year, and it never disappoints.



5]PlotTwistHunter
The Originals is intense, emotional, and unpredictable in the best ways. 
While the show occasionally gets too dramatic, the last few seasons are 
some of the most powerful storytelling I’ve seen in a supernatural series. 
The relationships feel raw, and the family theme is surprisingly deep. A 
great balance of dark fantasy and heartfelt moments.
