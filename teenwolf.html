Teen Wolf reviews




1] HowlingAtMidnight:
Teen Wolf is one of those shows that starts good
 and gets AMAZING. The character growth is 
everything—Scott’s transformation from awkward 
teen to true Alpha is so inspiring. Stiles absolutely 
steals every scene, and the supernatural creatures 
introduced each season keep things fresh. The mix 
of humor, horror, and heart is unbeatable. I still 
rewatch it whenever I miss Beacon Hills.



2]CasualLycan:
I enjoyed Teen Wolf, but it definitely had its 
ups and downs. Some seasons were top-tier, while 
others felt too rushed or confusing. The special 
effects aged weirdly too. But the chemistry between 
the cast, especially Scott and Stiles, kept me 
watching. A fun show, but not without flaws.



3]CriticalFang
Not impressed. The plotlines often felt inconsistent, 
and the show leaned too heavily on drama rather than 
actual horror. I also found some character arcs 
unresolved or abandoned. While the concept had potential, 
the execution didn’t hook me. Cool creatures though—that
 part was creative.



4]BeaconHillsForever
Absolute masterpiece for a supernatural teen series! 
The friendships, the loyalty, the humor—everything hit 
perfectly for me. The Nogitsune arc alone is worth the 
watch. Stiles is one of the best TV characters ever 
written, hands down. The show made me laugh, cry, and 
scream at my screen. I’ll always love it.



5]PlotSeeker101
Teen Wolf is chaotic, creepy, and surprisingly emotional 
in all the right ways. Some plot holes? Sure. But the 
suspense and character-driven moments more than make
 up for it. The villains are unique, and the mythologies 
are well thought out. Definitely one of the better 
supernatural teen dramas out there.
