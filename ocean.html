Ocean's 8 reviews





1] as_if_itsanisha:
Ocean’s 8 is a fun and entertaining heist movie. The 
all-female cast brings a fresh vibe to the Ocean’s 
series. The story is easy to follow, and the heist 
scenes are exciting. Some parts feel a little slow, 
but overall it’s enjoyable. It’s a good movie to 
watch with friends if you like crime and suspense.



2] allcool.kartik
Ocean’s 8 revitalizes the franchise with a stylish 
female-led cast, including Sandra Bullock, Cate Blanchett, 
and Anne Hathaway. The film excels in costume design, 
set pieces, and its slick tone. However, the stakes 
feel lower compared to earlier Ocean’s films, and the 
tension is often minimal. While the plot is 
predictable, the charisma of the cast and 
the smooth execution make it a fun, watchable heist movie.



3]blackloverboy3
I loved Ocean’s 8! The cast is amazing—every 
actress fits her role perfectly. 
The fashion, humor, and clever planning of the 
heist kept me hooked from start to finish. 
Anne Hathaway absolutely shines, and the whole 
movie feels glamorous and cool. It’s not super 
intense, but it’s very stylish and enjoyable. 
I’d totally recommend it for a fun movie night!



4]roshni_aturhelp
Ocean’s 8 offers an interesting commentary 
on the representation of women in heist 
cinema. The film subverts traditional 
gender roles by placing women at the center 
of a complex, high-risk operation. It 
also highlights themes of trust, loyalty, 
and strategy within an all-female team. 
While the narrative structure follows 
conventional heist tropes, the film’s 
focus on fashion, social status, and 
collaboration provides a modern reinterpretation of the genre.



5]praffulthebest
Ocean’s 8 is a stylish and enjoyable movie. The 
heist is fun to watch, and the actresses make the 
movie even better. It doesn’t have a lot of action, 
but the humor and teamwork keep it interesting. 
The Met Gala setting makes the film look beautiful. 
Overall, it’s a cool and light movie that’s perfect 
for relaxing.
