Cruella reviews





1] as_if_itsanisha:
Cruella is a visually stylish and fun movie. I loved 
the costumes, the music, and Emma Stone’s acting. 
The story shows how Estella becomes Cruella, and 
it’s interesting to watch her transformation. Some 
scenes are a bit dramatic, but overall the movie is 
entertaining and creative. It’s great for people who 
enjoy fashion and exciting characters.



2] allcool.kartik
Cruella is a bold reimagining of a classic Disney 
villain, driven by Emma Stone’s excellent performance. 
The film shines in its production design, costume artistry, 
and 1970s punk-inspired aesthetic. However, the narrative 
occasionally feels predictable and leans heavily on 
style over substance. Still, the film succeeds in delivering 
a unique origin story with strong visual appeal and great performances.



3]blackloverboy3
I absolutely loved Cruella! The fashion, the attitude, the 
music—everything was perfect. Emma Stone plays the role with 
so much energy and confidence that it’s impossible to look 
away. The rivalry between Cruella and the Baroness was super 
entertaining. The movie felt cool, stylish, and powerful. 
Definitely one of my favourite Disney live-action films!



4]roshni_aturhelp
Cruella offers an interesting study in character development, 
identity formation, and societal expectations. The film explores 
themes of class struggle, artistic expression, and moral ambiguity. 
The fashion-centric narrative reflects how style can function as 
a form of rebellion and self-definition. Additionally, the film 
reinterprets villainy through a psychological and socio-cultural 
lens, making it valuable for media and gender studies analysis.



5]praffulthebest

 are amazing. Emma Stone does a fantastic job, and the story is 
fun to follow. Some scenes are a little long, but the humour

like fashion and strong characters
