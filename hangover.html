The Hangover reviews





1] as_if_itsanisha:
The Hangover is one of the funniest movies I’ve seen. the 
story is crazy and unpredictable, and the characters make 
the movie even better. I kept laughing in almost every scene. 
It’s a light-hearted comedy that’s perfect for watching with 
friends. Some jokes are a bit adult, but overall it’s really entertaining.



2] allcool.kartik
The Hangover is a well-crafted comedy that cleverly combines 
chaos, mystery, and humor. The film’s structure—waking up with 
no memory and retracing the wild night—keeps the audience engaged 
throughout. Zach Galifianakis delivers a standout performance as Alan, 
contributing to the film’s unique comedic tone. While some jokes 
rely on shock value, the pacing, chemistry among the cast, and 
creative writing make it a memorable comedy.



3]blackloverboy3
I absolutely loved The Hangover! From start to finish, it’s hilarious. 
The storyline is wild, and every character adds something special. Alan, 
Stu, and Phil make the perfect chaotic team. The twists made me laugh out 
loud, especially when they finally find out what happened the night 
before. It’s one of my favorite comedy movies ever—super fun and entertaining!



4]roshni_aturhelp
The Hangover presents an interesting case study in modern comedic 
storytelling. The film uses the “lost memory” narrative to explore 
themes of masculinity, friendship, irresponsibility, and social expectations. 
Through exaggerated situations, it reflects how contemporary comedies 
rely on shock humor and fast-paced narrative structures. Despite its 
comedic tone, the film offers insight into group dynamics, personal identity, 
and the consequences of impulsive behavior.



5]praffulthebest
I enjoyed watching The Hangover because it’s funny and unpredictable. 
The characters get into crazy situations, and it keeps you guessing how 
they will fix everything. It’s not too serious—just a fun movie to relax with. 
Some scenes are silly, but that’s what makes it entertaining. A great choice 
if you like light comedy.
